FundCart Support

Q. How can I use FundCart from a mobile device?
Through this application, we have included a shopping option that takes you to a version of various companies where you can also donate to your specified charities. Simply use the menu-shop option and it will take you to a list of potential companies to shop from. Once you choose your option, purchase your desired items, then, in the checkout menu, you will be able to select a charity and amount to donate.

Q. How can I use FundCart from a desktop computer?
FundCart offers a web extension that works on non-cellular devices. You will find an option in the menu to download this extention. Once you have it, you will be offered the opportunity to donate money to charities from the checkout menu.

Q. Where can I learn more about the charity options?
FundCart offers a description of all potential charities in the "charities" section of the menu. From there, you could also navigate to the charities website for even more information about them.

Q. The charity I want to donate to isn't listed, what can I do?
We appreciate the enthusiasm and would love to hear from you. In order to recomment a new charity option, email us at support@FundCart.com. Important information would include: the name of the charity, what cause they support and where they do most of their work. We look forward to hearing from you!

Q. The extention does not work on my computer, what do I do?
If the extention is not displaying your donation options at checkout, try using a different browser, like google chrome. If the problem continues, you can email us at support@FundCart.com or use the chatbox option in the botton right corner of the screen to contact someone to help you.

Q. Why should I donate through FundCart instead of directly to the charities?
FundCart is a tool, designed to help people with busy lives, to still find the time to donate to causes that are important to them. Donating directly to charities is always good, but when you have little time, being able to combine tasks can save you a lot of trouble.
